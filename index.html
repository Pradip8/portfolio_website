Explain ETL vs ELT simply
Magic Guru
Great question! 🔥

ETL = Extract → Transform → Load. You clean data BEFORE it enters the warehouse. Old-school, suits structured data.

ELT = Extract → Load → Transform. Load raw data first, transform INSIDE the warehouse. Modern approach — perfect for Snowflake & BigQuery!
