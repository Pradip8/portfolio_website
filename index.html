ℹ This page explains how we perform each type of repair at Vodafone Bondi Junction. Visible to all staff.
📱 Screen Repair Process
✅
Customer Check-in & Assessment
Verify booking, document damage, check warranty status, quote price with promo code BONDI40.
⏱ 5–10 min
✅
Data Backup Prompt
Ask customer to back up device. Note serial number and IMEI for the ticket.
⏱ 5 min
🔵
Disassembly & Screen Removal
Heat device to loosen adhesive, use suction cup and spudger tools. Disconnect display cables carefully.
⏱ 15–20 min
⚪
Install Genuine Part
Fit certified OEM screen, reconnect all cables, test touch and display before sealing.
⏱ 15–25 min
⚪
Quality Check & Reassembly
Full functionality test — touch, face ID, brightness. Seal device. Apply screen protector.
⏱ 10 min
⚪
Customer Handover
Customer tests device in store. Issue 90-day warranty card. Process payment. SMS receipt.
⏱ 5 min
🔋 Battery Replacement Process
✅
Check-in & Battery Diagnostic
Run battery health check. Confirm battery is below 80% or customer experiencing issues.
⏱ 5 min
✅
Device Disassembly
Remove back panel or screen depending on device model. Locate battery connector.
⏱ 10–15 min
🔵
Battery Swap
Remove adhesive strips, lift old battery, install certified replacement. Reconnect cables.
⏱ 10 min
⚪
Reassemble & Test
Confirm charging, check battery percentage reads correctly. Full cycle test if time permits.
⏱ 10–15 min
💧 Water Damage Process
✅
FREE Diagnostic Check
Open device, inspect logic board for corrosion. Note damage level 1–5. Give honest prognosis.
⏱ 15–20 min
✅
Ultrasonic Cleaning
Submerge logic board in ultrasonic bath with isopropyl alcohol solution. Remove mineral deposits.
⏱ 30 min
🔵
Dry & Inspect
Air dry board. Inspect under microscope for corroded components or shorted capacitors.
⏱ 2–4 hours
⚪
Component Repair
Reball or replace damaged ICs. Micro-soldering if required. Not always possible for severe damage.
⏱ 1–3 hours
⚪
Test & Handover
Power on, test all functions. No warranty on water damage repairs as damage may recur.
⏱ 15 min
